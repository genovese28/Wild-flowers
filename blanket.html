Blanket Flower

  • Perennial
  • Plant in spring or fall
  • Full sun
  • They like average, moist, well-drained soil
  • Drought and salt tolerant
  • Best in zones 4 - 9
  • Bloom in the summer
  • Propigated with seeds, division in early spring, new growth cutting(late summer), root cutting(fall)
    • Start seeds indoors 6- 8 weeks before the last frost date or sow them directly into the ground in spring
    • Do not cover them, they need light
  • Plants will stay vigorous if divided every 2 to 3 years in the spring or fall.
  • Unlikley to survive in winer in heavy clay soil because they are susceptable of root rot
  • Soil that is too rich in nutrients or over fertilized soil will result in floppy, short-lived plants.
  • They tend to thrive in areas where nothing else seems to grow.
