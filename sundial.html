Sundial Lupine

  • Herbaceous perennial
  • It has clumps of leaves 12- 18 inches tall and enormous flowes that rise an additional 12-18 nches above the foilage
  • Found in Florida to Maine and west from Minnesota to Texas
  • Full sun to thrive
  • Reseed in sandy or well-drained soils
  • Sole host plant for the Karner butterfly, also the Frosted Elfin butterfly. It attracts hummingbirds, birds, and bees.
  • Enhances soil fertility by fixing atmospheric nitrogen into a useful form.
